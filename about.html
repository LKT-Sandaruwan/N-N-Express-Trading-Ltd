About N&N Express Trading Ltd

Your trusted wholesale partner since 2008

Our Story

Founded in 2008, N&N Express Trading Ltd started as a small family business in Colombo with a vision to provide quality wholesale products to retailers across Sri Lanka. What began with just 10 products has now grown into a comprehensive wholesale operation serving over 500 businesses.

Our Mission

To be Sri Lanka's most reliable wholesale distributor by providing quality products at competitive prices with exceptional customer service and timely delivery across the island.

Our Vision

To empower small and medium businesses across Sri Lanka by providing them access to quality wholesale products that help them grow and succeed in their respective markets.
